About Me

Hello! I’m Kanyuira Jakes, a Kenyan student currently pursuing a Master of Divinity at Chongshin University in South Korea. My journey is shaped by a deep passion for youth ministry, leadership development, and building bridges across cultures and communities. I believe that true change begins at the heart — through service, discipleship, and compassion.

Ministry Passion

My calling is rooted in the belief that young people, when equipped with truth, identity, and vision, can transform societies. Having worked in various cultural spaces, I’ve seen firsthand how faith becomes a unifying force — a common ground where healing, justice, and hope meet. From volunteering with multicultural communities in Korea to mentoring youth in Kenya, I seek to reflect Christ’s love in action. Ministry for me is not just in the pulpit, but in presence — showing up, listening, and walking alongside others.
