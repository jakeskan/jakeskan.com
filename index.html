About Me

I’m Kanyuira Jakes, a passionate servant-leader from Kenya currently pursuing a Master of Divinity at Chongshin University in South Korea. My focus lies in youth ministry, discipleship, and cross-cultural leadership. Through volunteering and intercultural engagement, I aim to create spaces where young people feel seen, valued, and empowered to rise.

Ministry Passion

Ministry, for me, is more than teaching — it’s relational, restorative, and reflective of Christ’s humility. I believe in the transformative power of intentional presence: listening, mentoring, and leading with authenticity. My heart beats for Africa's young generation, where I see visionaries, change-makers, and faithful witnesses rising in every nation.
